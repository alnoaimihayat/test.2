About Me

My Story:

Hello Everyone my name is Hayat AlNoaimi and ever since I was a young child my favorite hobby was drawing, especially using white and black colors only. I was inspired by my sisters who used to always create the most amazing and creative drawings and hang them on their bedroom walls. Needless to say, my family is my greatest supporters, always motivating me and allowing me to continue developing my drawing skills. Furthermore, my school teachers also played a major role in boosting my confidence and developing as an artist with their endless advice and compliments, which im very grateful for! Ever since they started hanging my drawings up on the school walls till this day, all I do in my free time is drawing. I decided to expand my creativity and techniques by developing a new idea which is to draw using charcoal. This project gave me confidence and strength to let me share this idea and my techniques with everyone I don't think I would have dared to come out of my comfort zone without it.
